About Us

"An oft-cited reason people don’t buy electric cars is “range anxiety” — if batteries struggle to take you as far as gas and charging stations are limited in number, the thinking goes, who would want one?" - New-York Times. With our service, we aim to increase the amount of EV vehicles on the road by eliminating the "range anxiety" factor. By implementing personal EV charging stations people can gain access to millions of charging stations nationwide and offer their own in return.

With over 1.25 million electric vehicle users, there is a strong demand for community chargers. EV drivers love the idea that they are reducing carbon emmision, and bettering our communities overall. One of the few problems EV drivers face, however, is lack of community charging stations. Sure there are more now than there have been in the past, but EV drivers consistantly worry about driving far, or for long periods of time so we are here to help.
